PROJECTS



MBA PROJECT: FINANCIAL STATEMENT ANALYSIS OF HANTEX (KSHWCS LTD) THIRUVANANTHAPURAM (2022)


The objective of this project was to find out efficiency of the organization using business financial ratios like profitability ratios, turnover ratio and solvency ratio of the organization through comparative analysis and to provide suitable suggestions for improving the business financial performance of the organization.

B-Tech PROJECT: MULTI-SOURCE LEAKAGE CURRENT PROTECTION FOR LOW AND MEDIUM VOLTAGE APPLICATIONS


We have developed an RCCB which will come into action if any fault occurs in the system after main supply fails and backup inverter is in action. A current transformer based residual current circuit breaker is proposed which can detect any fault in any condition.
